Project 1

HRIS data audit and troubleshooting

A portfolio project designed to show HRIS analyst work: auditing employee records, identifying data quality issues, prioritizing risk, and recommending process fixes that improve reporting reliability and payroll accuracy.

Project focus

CheckWhy it matters
Duplicate employee IDsProtects data integrity
Missing compensationReduces payroll and reporting risk
Invalid manager assignmentPreserves org hierarchy accuracy
Status / termination mismatchPrevents lifecycle and compliance errors

My thinking process

I created an HRIS database to show how I’d handle employee record quality in a real system.

I reviewed a sample dataset for high-risk exceptions, logged the issues, and recommended fixes and validation improvements.

I corrected the issues immediately and prevented them from happening again.
